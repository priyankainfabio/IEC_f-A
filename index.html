Welcome To IEC

IEC School of Art & Fashion

IEC School of Art & Fashion is one of Nepal’s leading creative institutions for fashion and interior education. It is designed for students who want practical, career-focused learning with exposure to the real creative industry.

Students learn through design assignments, studio practice, portfolio shoots, workshops, industry visits, fashion shows and mentorship.
